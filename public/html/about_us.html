History

Durian Organic Farm

Durian Organic Farm has experienced exceptional development since its inception in 2010, quickly establishing itself as the go-to source for speciality durian knowledge. Bryan Gan is the originator of this company, which has been in operation for about two decades.

Why is Durian Organic Farm?

The sensation that I was truly a part of the community was the first reason why owning my own Farm was fantastic. As simple as it was, conversing with our regulars over a cup of coffee in the morning was a wonderful experience and one of my favourites. Making durian to reflect the many stages of life was also a lot of fun, and it felt extra great to be able to help celebrate these milestones with our favourite visitors.
